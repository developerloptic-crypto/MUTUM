# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the handling of user information by the application "MUTUM" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"), distributed via GitHub.

## 1. Information Collection and Management
The App does not transmit, store, or collect any personal information, chat history, input text, or voice data to any developer-owned or third-party servers. All AI-related processing and data storage are executed and contained entirely within the user's local device environment.

## 2. Web Search Feature (Utilization of DuckDuckGo)
The App utilizes a Web search feature to retrieve up-to-date information for the AI.
* When a search is executed, the entered keywords are sent to DuckDuckGo (https://html.duckduckgo.com/html/), and the App parses the results locally.
* DuckDuckGo is a privacy-focused search engine that does not collect or track user personal information.
* The developer of the App does not collect, monitor, or view any search keywords entered by the user.

## 3. Access to Third-Party Websites (Deep RAG Feature)
To further analyze and summarize search results, the App connects directly from the user's device to the respective third-party websites to retrieve text content.
During this process, access logs (such as your IP address) may be recorded on the servers of those third-party websites, similar to standard web browsing. This is a direct consequence of standard network communication from your device and is not the result of the App intentionally transmitting data to the developer or any specific third party.

## 4. Changes to this Policy
The developer reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. The updated policy becomes effective immediately upon being posted to the GitHub repository or designated platform.
